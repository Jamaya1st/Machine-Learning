And this is my
Predictive Fare Model

Have you ever wonder what is cheaper when looking for a ride?
I'm sure you pull out your phone and check between Lyft and Uber assuming your taxi trip will be too much.
I have trained an application to check cost rather than taking a chance on a cab on high peaks of the day.
With this application you can input date, time, and distance to get a hig probable price with an error of a $1.
Below I will talk about the development of the model and it's current accuracy of 70%.
